About Us

Building brighter futures for children and communities.

Butiki Children’s Foundation is committed to improving the lives of children through education, care, and community-driven support.

Our goal is to create opportunities that help children stay in school, develop confidence, and access the support they need to become responsible future leaders.

Our Programs

What we do

Quality Education

Supporting children with learning opportunities, school needs, mentorship, and education-focused community programs.

Child Welfare

Helping vulnerable children access care, guidance, confidence, and a safer environment to grow.

Community Support

Working with families, schools, and local leaders to build stronger communities through shared responsibility.
